## FAQ

### What is Movila?
Movila generates beautiful mini-films from your life memories. Using AI, we transform family photos into cinematic heritage films that capture the journey, emotions, and memories of your loved ones.

### How many photos do I need?
We recommend 15-50 photos for the best results. You can include images from any era ‚Äî childhood, milestones, candid moments, and recent photos all work beautifully together.

### What types of photos work best?
Any photos that tell a story! Old black-and-white prints, polaroids, digital photos ‚Äî we handle them all. The more variety in ages and moments, the richer your mini-film becomes.

### How long does it take?
Most mini-films are ready within an hour. Rush orders available for special occasions.

### Is my family's data safe?
Absolutely. Your photos are encrypted during upload and processing, and we delete all files from our servers within 30 days of delivery.

### Can I make edits?
Yes! We offer one round of revisions included with every order.
